## What is Zapmail?

Zapmail is a disposable temporary email service that protects your real email address from spam, advertising mailings, and malware. It's anonymous, free, and requires no registration.

Built with a custom SMTP server written in Go and a modern Next.js interface, Zapmail provides a reliable, secure, and efficient way to receive emails without compromising your privacy.

## Common use cases

Zapmail is perfect for situations where you need an email address but want to protect your privacy:

- **Forum registrations** - Sign up for forums and discussion boards without exposing your real email
- **One-time verifications** - Verify accounts on websites you'll only use once
- **Free trial signups** - Access free trials without getting added to marketing lists
- **Wi-Fi access** - Register for public Wi-Fi that requires an email address
- **Download gated content** - Access whitepapers, ebooks, or resources that require email registration
- **Testing** - Developers can test email functionality without setting up complex infrastructure

<Note>
  While Zapmail works with most services, some platforms actively block temporary email domains. For critical accounts, we recommend using a permanent email address.
</Note>

## How it works

Zapmail operates on a simple yet powerful architecture:

1. **Custom SMTP server** - Built from scratch in Go, our SMTP server handles incoming emails efficiently
2. **PostgreSQL database** - Emails are stored securely in Supabase with automatic cleanup
3. **Next.js interface** - A modern web application provides instant access to your inbox
4. **Automatic deletion** - A cleanup job runs every hour to remove emails older than 7 days
